Who We Are

Stage Fright is an electrifying, up-and-coming rock band from Dallas, TX, blending modern rock energy with classic 70s vibes. Known for our powerful performances and authentic sound, we create music that resonates with every generation of rock lovers. The band came together in 2020 during late-night jam sessions in drummer Alex's garage. Bonded by their love for music and a shared dream, they've quickly risen to become one of the hottest new names in indie rock. Stage Fright is all about staying true to their roots, connecting with fans, and creating memories one show at a time.
